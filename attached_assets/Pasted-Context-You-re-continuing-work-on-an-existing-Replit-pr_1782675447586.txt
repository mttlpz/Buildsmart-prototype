STEP 5 — Quotation Generation (preserve, fix pricing order)
=====================================================================

Mostly preserve the current build. Correct the pricing pipeline to this 
exact order — this is a real logic fix, not a style choice, since 
discount math needs a price to discount:

  Fetch base price (by region/source priority)
        ↓
  Apply Supplier Discount Rules (requires a price to evaluate 
  minimum_order_amount/discount_percentage against — never apply before 
  fetch)
        ↓
  Apply Pricing Strategy markup %
        ↓
  [parallel] Apply Unit Conversion + wastage % to quantity
  [parallel] Apply Labor Rules to service cost (region + trade)
        ↓
  Combine into grand total

Retrieve Market Insights stays a read-only side panel the estimator can 
consult during this flow — it is NOT part of the price calculation chain 
itself.
