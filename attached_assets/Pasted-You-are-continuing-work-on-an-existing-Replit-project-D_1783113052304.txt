FIX 1 — Animated BuildSmart cube (left-side visual on auth pages)
=====================================================================

Create client/src/components/BuildSmartCube.tsx.

This component renders an isometric cube made of 48 
individual brick cells, matching the rounded-rectangle 
brick pattern in the BuildSmart logo (the orange cube 
visible in the uploaded Figma screenshots). The cube has 
three visible faces arranged isometrically — do not render 
it as a flat square grid.

Cell states:
- Unfilled: full orange color but opacity 10%
- Filled: full orange color, opacity 100%, with a short 
  fade-in transition (150ms ease-in) when it activates

The component accepts a prop: filledCells: string[] (an 
array of cell IDs). Any cell whose ID is in that array 
renders at full opacity.

Assign cells using this exact mapping so the cube fills 
visually from top-left to bottom-right as the user 
progresses:

Step 1 — Company Info:
  Logo uploaded:        cells A1 A2 B1 B2
  Company Name:         cells A3 A4 B3
  Office Phone Number:  cells A5 A6 B4
  Company Address:      cells B5 B6 C1 C2
  City/Municipality:    cells C3 C4
  Province/Region:      cells C5 C6

Step 2 — Contact & Access:
  Email Address:        cells D1 D2 D3
  Password valid:       cells D4 D5
  Passwords match:      cells D6 E1

Step 3 — Service Profile:
  Service Price regions (each one selected adds a cell):
                        cells E2 E3 E4 E5 E6
  Project Sector tags:  cells F1 F2 F3
  Company Role:         cells F4 F5
  Specialization tags:  cells F6 G1 G2

When all fields on all 3 steps are complete, all 48 cells 
are filled — cube appears fully solid, same as the logo.

The parent signup component tracks which fields have valid 
values and passes the corresponding cell IDs down to 
<BuildSmartCube filledCells={filledCells} />.
